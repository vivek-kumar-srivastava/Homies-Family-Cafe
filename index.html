ABOUT US

Homies Family Café is more than just a café — it’s a community hub built around comfort, creativity, and connection. Whether you’re here for our signature coffee, our weekend live music, or to learn something new in our cooking sessions, we promise a space that feels like home.

Please note: We are a cashless café. The kitchen closes at 10 PM with last orders at 9 PM.

Cooking Lessons

Join our cooking workshops and learn from experienced chefs how to prepare authentic Indian and continental dishes. Perfect for beginners or food enthusiasts looking to up their culinary game.

Bookings

Planning a cozy dinner, birthday, or family celebration? Reserve your table or book a private area for your next gathering — we make sure your moments become memories.

Events

We host live acoustic nights, open mics, and seasonal festivals to bring the community together. Stay tuned for upcoming events and themed nights at Homies!
